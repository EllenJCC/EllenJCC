About Me


My name is Ellen Cole-Clarke and I am a digital media artist from Sydney Australia. I specialise in 3D character modelling and animation as well as abstract film.

I have recently completed my Bachelor of Media Arts majoring in 3D animation, film and VFX. While studying I have also had the opportunity to explore physical mediums including ceramics, sculpture and darkroom photography.

Awards

2017 ATOM Awards Junior Student finalist in the 2D animation category for a 60s work on the Gender pay gap in Australia

2019 Nominated for the InTech showcase of “Outstanding” HSC Industrial technology (Multimedia) Major Projects for my work Thumbelina

2022 Inclusion in the UNSW Media Arts showcase for my 3D animation Metamorphosis
